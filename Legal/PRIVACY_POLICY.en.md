This Privacy Policy explains how the {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} app ("app", "{{ site.data.legal.app_name }}", "we") handles data. {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} is a local personal finance app. In the current version, {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} does not use server sync, cloud accounts, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs.

Privacy contact: antonparf8025@gmail.com.

## 1. Summary

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }} stores your financial data on your device. We do not receive, sell, or share your financial data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Some actions may move data outside the app only when you choose to do so, such as creating a backup, exporting an Excel file, sharing a file through the system share sheet, importing a file from another app, or contacting support.

## 2. Data You May Store In The App

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }} may store data on your device that you enter or create while using the app:

- profile data: display name, email, birth date, selected avatar, base currency, language, and local settings;
- accounts: account names, account types, currencies, balances, credit limits, savings goals, debts, notes, icons, colors, and display order;
- transactions: amounts, currencies, dates, categories, subcategories, source and target accounts, exchange rates, tags, and notes;
- categories and budgets: names, icons, colors, limits, budget months, and carry-over modes;
- app settings: theme, money display format, favorite currencies and rates, launch screen, and budget settings;
- local technical identifiers, record creation and update dates, change logs, and service data used to preserve local database integrity;
- backup files and export files, if you create them.

The app does not connect to bank accounts and does not ask for bank credentials.

## 3. Where Data Is Stored

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }}'s main data is stored locally in the app container on your device. Access is limited by iOS protections and your device security settings.

If you use backups or export, files are created on your device and then controlled by you. After you save or share such a file, its handling depends on the storage location or service you choose, such as Files, iCloud, a messenger, email, cloud drive, or another app.

Your device may also include {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} data in iOS or iCloud device backups if enabled in your device settings. Those processes are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

## 4. Biometric Protection

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }} may use Face ID or Touch ID to unlock the app locally. Biometric templates are handled by iOS and are not provided to {{ site.data.legal.app_name }}. The app stores only a local setting indicating whether Face ID or Touch ID protection is enabled.

## 5. When Data May Leave The Device

We do not send your financial data to a {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} server. Data may leave your device in these cases:

- you export data to Excel or create a backup and share the file through the system share sheet;
- you save a file to cloud storage or open it in another app;
- you import a backup from another app or cloud storage;
- you voluntarily send us information by email, support message, TestFlight feedback, or another communication channel;
- Apple processes data related to the App Store, TestFlight, purchases, installations, device diagnostics, or your Apple ID under Apple's own rules.

The open beta does not use a {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} account, backend, or sync. In future versions, we may add an optional account, backend, and sync across devices. Before those features are enabled, this Policy will be updated and the app will ask the user to accept the updated terms.

If a future version adds server sync, cloud accounts, analytics, crash reporting, payments, advertising SDKs, or other external services, this Policy will be updated before that version is released.

## 6. Third-Party Technology

In the current version, {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} uses GRDB.swift as a local SQLite database library. GRDB runs inside the app and does not receive your data.

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }} does not use ad networks, data brokers, third-party analytics SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs in the current version.

## 7. How Data Is Used

Local data is used to provide app functionality:

- tracking accounts, transactions, categories, budgets, and currencies;
- calculating balances, debts, savings, budgets, and financial summaries;
- importing, restoring, backing up, and exporting data;
- configuring appearance, language, formats, and local app lock;
- diagnosing storage and restore errors on the device.

If you contact support, the information in your message is used to respond to your request and troubleshoot the issue.

## 8. Retention And Deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, delete the app, or erase the device.

The app includes an action to delete user data. Before deletion, {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} may create a local safety backup to reduce the risk of accidental data loss. Such backups remain on the device until you delete them manually.

Deleting data in {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} does not delete files you previously exported, sent to others, saved in Files, iCloud, email, messengers, or other apps. Those copies must be deleted separately in the relevant services.

If you contacted support, you may ask us to delete your support messages where applicable and where retention is not required by law.

## 9. Security

{{ site.data.legal.app_name }} is designed as a local-first app. We rely on iOS system protections, the app container, and optional biometric app unlock.

Backups and Excel exports may contain financial information. Store and share those files carefully. After export, the file's security depends on the location you choose, your device passcode, and the settings of iCloud, email, messaging, or other services.

No storage method can be completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, Apple ID, backups, and exported files.

## 10. Your Rights And Choices

Because {{ site.data.legal.app_name }} does not receive your local financial data, most data actions are handled directly on your device:

- you can view, edit, or delete records in the app;
- you can create backups or exports in the backup section;
- you can delete local data in profile settings;
- you can delete the app and its local data using iOS.

If you sent us an email or support request, you can contact antonparf8025@gmail.com to request access, correction, or deletion of data you voluntarily provided to us.

Depending on your country or region, you may have additional rights regarding personal data. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable law.
